Personal Details

Here are some more info about me

Hasan is very interested in Data Analytics, Data Science, and Business Intelligence by the belief that data can be used to make better decisions. He is a final year student at Universitas Sebelas Maret. He loves learning data visualization, data mining, and machine learning. He wants to be able to make it easier for people to understand data. He primarily uses R and Python, but also has experience with Matlab and SQL. Hasan has worked at e-learning company. By the way, check out my awesome projects.

Besides doing the final project, Hasan also is a volunteer for #GenerasiBebasPlastik.
